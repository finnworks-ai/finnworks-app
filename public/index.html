Performance & Speed

Load time and Google PageSpeed scores for both mobile and desktop — and exactly what's slowing you down.

SEO Foundations

Title tags, meta descriptions, headings, and image alt text — the basics Google uses to rank your site.

"A missing or duplicated page title is one of the most common — and easiest — SEO issues to fix."
📱

Mobile Experience

How your site performs for the 60%+ of visitors who are on a phone or tablet.

"If your site isn't optimised for mobile, Google ranks it lower for everyone — not just phone users."

Content & Messaging

Does your homepage explain what you do within 5 seconds? Is there a clear call to action?

"Visitors decide whether to stay or leave within 8 seconds. Your messaging needs to work fast."
🛡️

Trust & Credibility

HTTPS, privacy policy, phone number, testimonials — signals that turn cautious visitors into customers.

"Missing a privacy policy? Browsers now warn visitors on sites that don't have one."
🤖

GEO / AEO

Is your site structured for AI search? Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity pull from sites with clear FAQ content and schema markup.

"Sites with FAQ schema and a clear entity description are 3x more likely to appear in AI-generated answers."
📍

Local SEO

Will your site appear when people search for services in your area? Address, maps, and local schema are all checked.

"Most local searches include 'near me' — your site needs to tell Google where you are and what you do."
🎯

Conversion & UX

Can a visitor easily contact you, book, or buy? Checks forms, phone in header, contact page, and booking widgets.

"A site can rank #1 on Google and still fail to convert if the phone number is buried in the footer."
♿

Accessibility

Basic checks for alt text, form labels, heading structure, and ARIA roles — for all visitors, not just those with disabilities.

"Under the NZ Human Rights Act, public-facing websites should be reasonably accessible. Most aren't."
